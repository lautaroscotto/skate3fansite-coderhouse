Secret spots at the University district

Get back to school with this amazing map. Banks with ledges, huge Hubbas, a couple curved monster set of stairs and a whole lot more going on at the sickest skate university in the world.

Secret spots at the Industrial district

Be creative and found out all the tricks you can do arround factories and loading ships. Factory fun with lots of pipes to grind and warehouses to get lost in. Be sure to hit the two mega rainbow rails.

Secret spots at the Downtown district

Comprised of multiple high rise buildings and high end apartments. Once again the elevation in the terrain and the layout of the area it self is well-suited for skating with spots ranging from pedestrian walkways centered around skaters to purpuse built skate pools.
